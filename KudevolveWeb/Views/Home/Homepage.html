Overview

“Ku-Devolve” is an interactive, informative and engaging social reform dialogue mobile phone and web application. The platform is designed to disseminate critical information on county governance and fiscal structures, encourage dialogue on county activities and enhance accountability within county governments through a medium that is easily accessible, relatable, user friendly and current. The application intends to build capacity of the Kenyan people to comprehend, interact and engage with devolved functions of government. It aims to evolve and develop into a strong medium for change in governance through participative learning, idea sharing and collective action.

Launch Dashboard

Core Principles:

  • Simplicity
  • Social
  • Inspirational
  • Informative
  • Motivational

The relationship between the educational component and interactive component is bidirectional. In order for the application to be effective as a platform for interaction people need to understand the technicalities behind the new county governments. The premise behind this relationship is that once a user understands the county government structure they are equipped to address issues within their county using the interactive component.
